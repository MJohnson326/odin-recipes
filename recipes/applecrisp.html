Apple Crisp

This apple crisp recipe is both easy and delicious.

Learn how to make a simple fall favorite apple crisp. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream for an extra special treat.

Impress your guests and your family with this simple and delicious fall comfort dessert.

Ingredients

  • Apples: Ten cups peeled, cored, and sliced apples.
  • Sugar: One cup white sugar.
  • Flour: One tablespoon all-purpose flour.
  • Cinnamon: One teaspoon ground cinnamon.
  • Water: One-half cup (1/2c) water.
  • Oats: One cup quick-cooking oats.
  • Flour: One cup all-purpose flour.
  • Brown sugar: One cup packed brown sugar.
  • Baking powder: Quarter teaspoon (1/4tsp) baking powder.
  • Baking soda: Quarter teaspoon (1/4tsp) baking soda.
  • Butter: One-half cup (1/2c) melted butter.

Steps

  1. Mix: Combine white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, and cinnamon together.
  2. Combine: Combine oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Add melted butter and mix with fork until crumbly.
  3. Assemble: Place apples in a 9x13-inch baking dish, sprinkle sugar mixture over apples. Pour water evenly over apples. Sprinkle oat mixture evenly on top.
  4. Bake: Bake in 350 degree F preheated oven until top is golden brown and apples are bubbling at edge-around 45 minutes.
